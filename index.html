Who I Am

Music First, Curiosity Always

I'm a singer who found her voice through covers — Bollywood romance, Sufi longing, and folk roots — reinterpreted in my own tone and phrasing rather than performed as imitation.

Beyond music, I co-founded and help build three ventures alongside my partner: LyroWeb Solutions (digital services), Mr. Lyricist Distributor (music label & distribution), and Sohima Production (cinematic content studio). Alongside all of this, I'm currently pursuing Zoology Honours and an ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) in college — singing stays the constant thread through it all.
